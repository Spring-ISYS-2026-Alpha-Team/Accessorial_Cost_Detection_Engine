About

Accessorial charges often arrive after shipment completion, when it’s too late to prevent them— causing margin erosion, pricing surprises, and operational fire drills.

PACE solution

Predictive analytics turn historical shipment and facility patterns into proactive insights. PACE flags high-risk moves early and provides cost estimates so teams can adjust appointment type, carrier, routing, and buffers before execution.

Features

Decision support built for dispatch, pricing, and operations.

Risk Prediction

ML-powered accessorial probability scoring, tiered into Low / Medium / High risk.

Cost Estimation

Expected dollar impact calculations based on historical averages and shipment context.

Carrier Analytics

Performance insights by carrier, including risk trends, accessorial rates, and comparisons.
